Anmol Saving & Credit Co-oprative ltd.

Anmol Savings and Loan Cooperative Society Ltd. is a cooperative society located in Suryavinayak Municipality, Ward No. 8 of Bhaktapur District, which is rich in historical, social and cultural heritage.According to the three pillar economic policy adopted by the Government of Nepal, based on the principle of equality and democratic values with the objective of making the society economically prosperous through social and economic transformation. Established in 2066 BS, the scope of work of this organization is all over Bhaktapur district.

The organization collects the unproductive capital scattered within its area of work as a deposit to build investable capital and invests the capital according to the skills and abilities of its members in various productive, service oriented, for-profit businesses and other sectors. The organization aims to contribute to the upliftment of the living standards of the people by making full use of its resources, means and capabilities.
